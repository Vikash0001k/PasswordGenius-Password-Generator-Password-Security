Common Password Threats

Brute Force Attacks

Automated attempts to guess passwords by trying multiple combinations

Protection: Use long, complex passwords with mixed characters

Phishing Attacks

Deceptive attempts to steal passwords through fake websites or emails

Protection: Enable two-factor authentication

Data Breaches

Large-scale theft of password databases from companies

Protection: Use unique passwords for each account

Impact of Password Breaches

Business Impact

  • Financial losses averaging $4.35M per breach
  • Reputation damage and lost customer trust
  • Legal and regulatory consequences
  • Operational disruption

Personal Impact

  • Identity theft and financial fraud
  • Personal data exposure
  • Social media account compromise
  • Email security breaches
